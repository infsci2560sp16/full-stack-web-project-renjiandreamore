Orange Chicken

Chicken:
2 tablespoons cornstarch; 4 egg whites; 4 boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces;
Sauce:
1/2 cup orange juice (I used Simply Orange); 1 tablespoon soy sauce

For the chicken: In a large bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and egg
whites with a fork until almost frothy, about 1 minute. Add the chicken to the
mixture and allow to sit for 5 to 10 minutes.
Heat about 2 inches of vegetable oil in a heavy-bottomed Dutch oven until
a deep-fry thermometer inserted in the oil registers 350 degrees F. Then drop
them back into the oil for 1 minute
to really solidify the coating.
Toss the chicken in the sauce and serve.
